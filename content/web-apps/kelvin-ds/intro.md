Before any system work, I reviewed what was already in place: what each product was doing well, where the friction was, and what workflows users had already built their SOPs around.

That last part mattered most.

In a regulated environment, you cannot change an interface without understanding what processes depend on it.

The design system had to account for what already existed, not replace it.

That is what Kelvin is built around.
